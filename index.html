ShopLens — Visual AI Shopping

ShopLens lets you upload an outfit photo and instantly get adorably accurate matches from the catalog. This MVP explored whether reducing choice overload with a playful, “Top 3” layout could lift add‑to‑cart. We shipped a full slice—ingestion → matching → UI—then ran a controlled experiment and qualitative sessions.

User Research (n=30)

  • “Under 10 seconds feels instant” → kept p50 < 8s.
  • “Do these really match?” → added color/material badges.
  • Size confusion → auto‑preselect from history (editable).
  • “Find similar” copy won by +6% CTR over “Shop the look”.

How it works

  2. We compute a tiny HSV histogram via Canvas.
  3. We compare it to precomputed histograms from products.json and show the top‑3.
